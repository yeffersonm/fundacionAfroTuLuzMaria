the fundation “ESTA ES TU LUZ MARIA” is an organization of common utility, non-profit, at the service of the community open to all people. In the development of this fundamental purpose, the foundation may direct, plan, execute, coordinate, evaluate, organize and perform all kinds of activities of interest in society, mainly in management and purposes related to social service, competitiveness, recreation , sports, prevention in health, protection of children and the elderly, the environment, agricultural activities, technological development, employment generation, training for peace.

Social Responsibility (RSE)

Advising and accompaniment to develop social investment, we guide our clients so that they contribute actively and voluntarily with their business activity to social and economic improvement. Our advice is focused on:

Diagnosis of CSR in the company.

Development and implementation of CSR Management Models.

Advice on CSR certification processes.

Realization of the corporate social responsibility report.
